The Xbox is Microsoft's first home video game console, released during the sixth generation of video games. There are a total of 989[a] titles on this list. This list does not include Xbox Live Arcade games, demos, or bonus discs. For games that were announced or revealed to be in development for the original Xbox, but never released, see the list of cancelled Xbox games.

For a chronological list, click the sort button in any of the available region's column. Games dated November 15, 2001 (NA), February 22, 2002 (JP), and March 14, 2002 (PAL) are launch titles of each region respectively.
